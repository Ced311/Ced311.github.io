Reward 1

Upon opening box number 1 you are instantly teleported in a blinding flash of white light. Once your vision recovers from the sudden flash, you now find yourself in a dim and eerily lit hallway. The wall are ragged and worn, and as you start to explore, you start to realize that the setting is much more maze-like than you'd originally though. You tread this maze for what seems like an eternity, seeing various caution signs on the way, Though at this point your caution starts to waiver a bit. It waivers up until this very moment when you turn yet another corner,finding an oddly shaped figure, with beaming read eyes, standing motionless in the middle of the hallway. You step back reflexively, eyes glued to this ominous statue-like figure, until your eyelids naturally close in a blink. In that moment all you can hear is a loud earspliting snap. You never open your eyes again.

Return



































Reward 2

Upon opening box number 2 you are instantly teleported in a blinding flash of white light. Your new setting is now a silent and dimly lit corridor. After taking a moment to reorient yourself and get feel for the enviornment, you hesitantly start to tread the corridor. Upon further inspection the corridor is more futuristic than you'd originally though. You come to a doorway at the end of it, and attempt to open it. Locked, and suddenly you hear an intense hissing noise coming from behind you. You quickly turn to see absolutely nothing. Now in fight or flight, your vision darts sporadically around the corridor, trying to find the source. You then hear the once locked door shift open behind you. You turn around, and standing in the doorway before you is a large acid drooling monstrosity. Before you can even leverage you feet to run the creature lungs at you at blinding speed. It opens its maw and bites into your frontal lobe, ending you quickly. You are now the prey of a xenomorph.
